Who am I?

I am a Master of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I completed my undergradaute degree in Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I'm currently interested in studying privacy theory and differential privacy. I have a passion for both coding and mathematics, so I'd like to see if I can combine those in my future studies. Longterm, I hope to get a PhD to further my studies in security and privacy.

I am an amateur concert pianist and in my free time I try to play as much piano as I can. While I don't have the opportunity to play as much as I did in the past, I still enjoy performing and competing. I recently finished recording my second album and it should be on Spotify soon.
